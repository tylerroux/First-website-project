Overview

Both the mustang and camaro are sports cars. You can have either with a manual transmission or automatic transmission. Both of these cars compete with one another and have ever since they have been produced. The mustang was produced in 1964 while the camaro was first produced in 1967. These are two iconic cars that most people know of. The camaro is a chevrolet while the mustang is ford.

The mustang is now offered in with a 4 cylinder with a turbo or a 5.0 V8. The horsepower ranges from 310 hp to 480 hp. They also have special mustangs which are shelby mustangs and you can also have a roush mustang, these are both special performace vehicles.These offer more horsepower from 480-760.

The camaro is offered with a 4 cylinder with turbo, 6 cylinder, and a 8 cylinder. The horsepower ranges from 275-650. That is with the special model which is a ZL1. The camaro has more variety of engines while the mustang does not. It is usually cheaper than the mustang, but it is pretty close overall. Has slightly better technology and has more comfort. The camaro was built more for the track.
